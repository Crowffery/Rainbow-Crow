Optical Reflection

Unlike pigment-based color, the Rainbow Crow's vibrant hues are structural. Thin-film interference on the microscopic barbs of the feather creates a shifting spectrum that reacts to the environment.

Our brand leverages this optical truth: the deeper the darkness, the more vivid the light appears.
